Not Your Moms Pot Roast

Pot roast is a beloved American recipe. Delicious meat, lots of spices and herbs, and a touch of enticing sweetness; what’s not to like?

But what if you could take it a step further and add a captivating new dimension? Look no further! We have just the solution—infusing your beloved pot roast with cannabis!

Incorporating cannabis into your pot roast recipe elevates it to a whole new level of enjoyment. With this cannabis infusion, you'll unlock a brand new experience that delights your palate and adds an exciting element to your dining occasions.

This simple recipe, dreamed up by RISE Nevada's Trevor Voight, can satisfy your palate, uplift your mood, and make your time with friends so much more exciting!

Ingredients:

  • 2-2.5lb Chuck Roast
  • Small Bunch of Green Onion
  • 1 small-medium Shallot
  • 2 TBS – Minced Garlic
  • 1 ½ Water or Favorite Grilling Liquid
  • ½ Cannabis-Infused BBQ Sauce
  • Apple Cider Vinegar for Spritz
  • 2 TBS – Salt, Pepper, Garlic
  • ½ TBS – Smoked Paprika

Steps:

  1. Set smoker to 225°
  2. If needed, tie your Chuck Roast to ensure consistent thickness throughout
  3. Mix all ingredients to make the rub and rub the meat liberally. You can use a binder such as mustard or just a light coating of oil to ensure the rub sticks to the roast
  4. Smoke directly on the grate for 2.5 – 3 hours or until internal temperature reaches 165-170 degrees, spritzing with 50/50 Apple Cider Vinegar and water/favorite grilling liquid every 30 minutes
  5. Then, place into a foil pan (or large hotel pan) with the onion, shallot, garlic, cannabis-infused BBQ sauce (made by following your favorite BBQ sauce recipe and substituting regular honey for canna-honey), and water/grilling liquid
  6. Cover tightly with foil and return to smoke
  7. Continue cooking at 225° degrees until internal temperature reaches 205°. Remove pan from heat and let rest for at least 30 minutes but recommend 1 hour to ensure the roast is tender
