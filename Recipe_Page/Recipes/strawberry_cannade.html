StrawBerry Cannade

If you love lemonade, you can add one or two twists to it with this fabulous strawberry canna-nade recipe. It´s frozen, incredibly refreshing, and also exhilarating. Combining vitamin-rich fruits like strawberries with all the wonderful health benefits of cannabis, this canna-nade is a healthy and refreshing drink, ideal for a hot summer day.

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz. frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water or lemonade
  • 6 cups ice; more as needed
  • 10 mg Doctor Solomon’s Tincture
  • Blender
  • Large cocktail glasses

Steps:

  1. Add frozen strawberries, sugar, and lemonade/water into a blender and blend until evenly mixed
  2. Add cannabis tincture and ice to your mixture and blend, blend, blend!
  3. Serve in your fanciest cocktail glasses and enjoy!
